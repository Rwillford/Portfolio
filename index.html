Hello, my name is Robert Willford but you can just call me Bob. I'm the son of an IT technician. I have been happily married to an awesome wife Jamie now for almost 9 years come July 2022. We have three amazing children, ages 15, 7 and 6. As for work, I have over 12 years of experience being a Painter. Right now, I am a Lead Painter for Brush Master's.
